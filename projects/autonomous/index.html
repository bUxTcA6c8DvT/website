Autonomous

Your personal control panel

The Product

Autonomous is a fully customized personal home control panel. It's meant to be used on a wall-mounted tablet. Autonomous has many features and scenes based on your day and whatâ€™s important to you. Through Autonomous you can control all your devices as well as view the weather and see camera timelines. The goal of Autonomous was to bridge the features of many existing smart home devices into a single panel that allows for more customization, ease of use benefits, and better tracking.

Market Opportunities

Several company offerings were compared such as the Home Assistant, Google Home, Hubitat, along with those of Lutron and Wemo.

Market Opportunities:

  • Existing applications do not compartmentalize devices or actions well (Home Assistant)
  • Existing applications make it difficult to search when there are lots of devices (Google Home)
  • Existing applications lack logging and activity monitoring (Hubitat)
  • Limited interoperability results in limited use cases (Lutron, Wemo)

The User

The User is defined as a technology Superuser:

Tech savvy user

The Solution

  • Provide groups of devices as a shortcut selection. (Doors, lights, etc.)
    Groups in the form of chips are included to easy the selection process when there are a large number of devices available
  • Provide a timeline for camera activity with easy access to snippets of activity (camera)
    Camera activity is shown where it makes best use of the medium, as a timeline with activity points shown throughout.
  • Provide pre-coded home automation tasks (scenes)
    Scenes provide customizable image specific daily routines as you prepare for bed, for TV time, or any other routine as you go about your day.
  • Provide for multiple devices (thermostat)
    Autonomous expects multiple devices and has features for them built in by default.
  • Provide stats and activity for review (logs)
    Want to make sure everything is working? Logs provide you with a sense of closure and allow you to check on all activity posted by the control panel.

Conclusion

There are many competitors for this product out there that just miss what was sought after in this design. Within such a crowded space it can be hard to stand out, but this design has a unique appearance along with a unique feature set. Likely the most difficult aspect of creating this design will be encoding the camera timelines. To ensure this goes well, next steps would be to ensure that users are happy with the projected offering.
